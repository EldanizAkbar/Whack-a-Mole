Whack-a-Cell Game

The essence of the game is as follows: any unlit cell in the table briefly turns blue. You must click on the highlighted cell within the given time. If you manages to do so, the cell turns green, and the user earns 1 point. If not, the cell turns red, and the computer earns 1 point. The game continues until half of the cells on the grid are colored green or red. Once this happens, the player (human or computer) with more colored cells wins.
